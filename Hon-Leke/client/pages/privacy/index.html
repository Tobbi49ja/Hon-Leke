Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Privacy Policy · Terms of Service · Cookie Policy · Disclaimer
Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the official website of Rt. Hon. Elder Leke Joseph Abejide, Member of the House of Representatives, Yagba Federal Constituency, Kogi State, Nigeria ("we", "our", or "this website") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit leke-abejide.onrender.com.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily submit through our website, including:

  • Contact Form: Your full name, email address, subject, and message when you contact us.
  • Newsletter Subscription: Your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Comments: Your name, email address, and comment text when you leave a comment on a blog post.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit this website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • General geographic location (country or city level)
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)

1.3 Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser cookies and localStorage to store your preferences. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Responding to messages and enquiries submitted via the contact form
  • Sending newsletter updates you have subscribed to receive
  • Displaying your approved comments on blog posts
  • Improving the content, functionality, and user experience of this website
  • Understanding how visitors use the website through analytics

We do not sell, trade, rent, or transfer your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our server hosted on Render.com. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

4. Third-Party Services

This website may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts — for typography rendering
  • jsDelivr CDN — for loading JavaScript libraries
  • Cloudinary — for image hosting and optimisation
  • Render.com — for website hosting

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers.

5. Links to External Websites

This website may contain links to external websites, including our official Facebook and Instagram pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our records
  • Withdraw consent for newsletter communications at any time by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at ayanisolomon1@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: ayanisolomon1@gmail.com
  • Office: Yagba Federal Constituency, Kogi State, Nigeria
  • Website: Contact Page
