About Rt. Hon. Leke Abejide

Member, House of Representatives · Yagba Federal Constituency · Chairman, House Committee on Customs & Excise

Rt. Hon. Elder Leke Joseph Abejide

ADC · Member, House of Representatives

Rt. Hon. Elder Leke Joseph Abejide was born on 8th of May, 1975, to his parents from Alu, Yagba East Local Government Area of Kogi State. He grew up in ALU community, where his leadership traits were discovered early in life.

He obtained his primary school leaving certificate at LSMB Primary School, Alu-Igbagun, and later attended ALU Community Secondary School in 1991 — where his exceptional performance led to his appointment as Assistant Senior Prefect.

He proceeded to the prestigious Ahmadu Bello University Zaria, where he bagged his BSc. Economics from the Department of Economics, Faculty of Social Sciences. He further obtained a Masters Degree in Economics from Bayero University (BUK) Kano.

After his academic pursuits, he built a formidable career starting with DIVA-ITH-TIC-LTD-STI, Turkey (2004–2007). He is the Chairman of ABYEM INT'L LTD, a private clearing company, and an active member and patron of the Association of Clearing and Forwarding Agents.

Out of his demanding schedule, he founded the Leke Abejide Foundation — a nonpartisan NGO serving as an intervention agency for the less privileged, widows, aged people, and indigent students.

Hon. Leke Abejide contested and won his first election under the African Democratic Congress (ADC) in February 2019, and was re-elected for a second term in February 2023. He currently serves as Chairman, House Committee on Customs and Excise.

Key Achievements & Projects

A record of service, community development, and people-centered leadership in Yagba Federal Constituency.

🎓

WAEC Fees Payment

Paid WAEC fees for all students in public schools across Yagba Federal Constituency, amounting to over ₦43 Million Naira.

🏥

Health Center Renovation

Renovated health centers in collaboration with prominent indigenes, improving healthcare access for the community.

👮

Police Station Construction

Constructed a "C" Division police station in Alu, equipped with motorized borehole, generator, and fence.

💡

Rural Electrification

Donated poles for the rural electrification of the Igbo-Ero electricity project, bringing light to communities.

👩‍👩‍👧

Widow Empowerment

Continuous empowerment of widows since 2014, providing financial support and improving livelihoods.

💼

Youth Employment

Employed over 200 Yagba indigenes directly in his personal company ABYEM INT'L LTD.

📚

School Fees Support

Paying school fees from JSS to SSS for students in Alu colleges since 2014.

🏛️

Legislative Leadership

Serving as Chairman, House Committee on Customs and Excise — advocating for better trade policy in Nigeria.

Chief Mrs. Esther Modupe Abejide

Beloved Wife & Philanthropist

Chief Mrs. Abejide is a loving wife and mother, a pillar of strength and support for her husband and family, and an inspiration to women and girls across Nigeria.

Her six years of dedication to the empowerment of widows in Yagba Federal Constituency have undoubtedly changed the lives of countless women. She has also worked to improve access to quality education, provided health facilities, and built boreholes across Yagbaland.

Chief Mrs. Abejide has been dedicated to providing financial support to the less privileged and to students who need help with their education.
